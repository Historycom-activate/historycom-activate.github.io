History.com/activate


Enter your activation code to start watching History on your TV!


From documentaries to historical dramas, the History Channel offers a plethora of content that keeps audiences engaged and informed. If you're looking to access History Channel content through your streaming device, you'll need to activate your subscription first. Through "History.com/activate" you can link your existing cable or streaming TV subscription. This unlocks a wider range of content beyond what's freely available, including full episodes, specials, and exclusive web content.


Supported Devices:

  • Smart TVs
  • Streaming devices (Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV, etc.)
  • Gaming consoles (PlayStation, Xbox, etc.)

How to get the activation code?

  • Open the History app on your TV or streaming device.
  • Sign in with your cable provider or create a new account.
  • Look for the activation code on your screen. It may appear as:
    • Activate Your Device
    • Get Your Activation Code
    • Enter Code to Activate
  • Write down the code or take a screenshot of it.

Steps to Activate Your Device

  • Ensure that your device is connected to internet.
  • Now you need to download the History Channel app on your streaming device. If you're accessing from a desktop or laptop, simply navigate to "History.com/activate".
  • Open the History Channel app on your device.
  • Sign in with your cable provider or create a new account.
  • You will see an activation code displayed on your screen.
  • Note this code down, as you will need it for the next steps.
  • Go to "History.com/activate" on your computer or mobile device.
  • Enter the activation code displayed on your TV.
  • Click "Activate" to proceed.
  • To complete the activation process, you will need to sign in with your TV provider credentials.
  • Follow the prompts to complete the activation process.

Once you have successfully entered your TV provider details, your device will be activated. You can now start exploring and watching your favorite historical content, from riveting documentaries to fascinating series that bring history to life.


Explore History

Once your device is activated, dive into our extensive library of content:

  • Documentaries: Explore in-depth documentaries on a wide range of historical topics.
  • Series: Follow engaging historical series and learn about events that shaped the world.
  • Special Features: Access special features and bonus content available only to activated users.
